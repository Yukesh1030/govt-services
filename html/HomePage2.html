Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about Stackly2 services and urbanization.

How do I apply for a new Building Permission?

All building permissions are now processed through the unified TG-bPASS portal. You can start your application by clicking "Building Permission" in our services section or visiting the TG-bPASS website directly.

What is a TDR Certificate and how to get it?

Transferable Development Rights (TDR) allow property owners to separate development rights from their land. These have been fully digitized. You can apply for or digitize your existing TDR certificates through our online portal.

Can I book government venues for private events?

Yes, premium venues like Necklace Road, People's Plaza, and various Party Zones can be booked online. Use the "Venue Booking" service to check availability and pay the required fees.

What are the office hours for Stackly2 Head Office?

The Head Office is open Monday through Saturday from 10:00 AM to 5:30 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. However, our online portal is available 24/7 for all citizen services.
