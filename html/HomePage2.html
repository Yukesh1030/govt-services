Welcome to HMDA

The jurisdiction of Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority (HMDA) was formed by G.O.Ms.No.570 MA & UD (I1) Dept., dt.25.08.2008 of the then combined state of Andhra Pradesh Government in the year 2008 with an area of 7,257 Sq Km.

The Hyderabad Metropolitan Region has been extended up to the upcoming Regional Ring Road (RRR) covering a buffer area of upto(2)kms outside the RRR which is an expressway connecting National and State Highways having an length of 354 kms approximately and the formation of RRR is expected to see rapid growth of urbanization around it, in the foreseeable future, with concomitant rapid growth and development in the area up to RRR.

The Government has issued orders G.O. Ms. No. 68 MA & UD dt.:12-03-2025 to extend the boundaries of HMR up to the RRR, covering a buffer area of upto (2) kms and the Hyderabad Metropolitan Region (HMR) covering (1355) Revenue Villages from (104) Mandals in eleven (11) Districts with an area of about 10472.723 Sq.Kmts.
