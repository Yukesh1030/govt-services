How It Works

Apply in 4 Simple Steps

All Stackly2 online services follow a unified application workflow.

01

Register

Create your citizen account on TG-bPASS or Stackly2 Portal using Aadhaar / mobile number

02

Fill Application

Select the required service, fill the form, and upload documents (site plan, NOC, etc.)

03

Pay Fee

Pay applicable government fees online through UPI, net banking, or debit/credit card

04

Download

Track status in real time. Download your digitally signed certificate once approved
