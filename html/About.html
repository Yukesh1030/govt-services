Establishment & Jurisdiction

  • The Stackly Metropolitan Region (SMR) was declared by the Government in 2008 vide G.O.Ms.No.570 MA, dt: 25.08.2008 which included 849 villages under the Stackly Metropolitan Development Act 2008.
  • The Stackly Metropolitan Development Authority (SMDA) was constituted in 2008 vide G.O.Ms.No.571 MA, dt: 25.08.2008 for the SMR.
  • The Stackly Urban Development Authority (SUDA), Regional Development Authority (RDA), Airport Development Authority (ADA) & Central Project Authority (CPA) were dissolved and merged into SMDA.
  • The SMR jurisdiction earlier covered 70 Mandals in 7 Districts with 1059 Villages covering an area of 7257 Sq.Kms.
  • The Government has notified a "Special Area Development Authority" named as "Future City Development Authority" (FCDA) for the development of "Future City Area" consisting of 56 Revenue Villages comprised in 7 Mandals spread over 765.28 Sq.Kms vide G.O.Ms.No. 69 MA & UD Dept.
  • The Government has excluded 36 Revenue Villages from the existing SMR for inclusion of the said Villages in the FCDA and declared the SMR covering 1355 Revenue Villages comprised in 104 Mandals in 11 Districts with an area of about 10,472.723 Sq.Kms vide G.O.Ms.No. 68 MA.
